About Us




Welcome To S&K Hotels




A 5-Star Luxurious Stay in Palakkad

_Come experience the Expression of happiness satisfaction and contentment _



Nestled along the serene banks of the Kalpati River, Palakkad's S&K Hotels extend a warm welcome to both business and leisure travelers, offering luxurious amenities and 5-star accommodations in a prime location near the industrial hub and downtown area. It opens the door to the world of scenic beauty. Guests can enjoy a hassle-free mobile check-in before settling into their well-appointed rooms or suites, complete with complimentary WiFi, comfortable bedding, and modern amenities tailored for today's traveler. Delight in unique culinary experiences featuring authentic local and international cuisines at our distinctive dining venues, relax by the outdoor pool, rejuvenate at the spa, or maintain your fitness routine at our 24-hour fitness center. Our elegant ballroom and versatile meeting spaces ensure that every wedding or social gathering is a memorable and personalized affair. For those seeking an unforgettable wedding celebration, our lawn and open terrace provide an idyllic setting. Whether you're visiting for leisure or business, we eagerly await the opportunity to host you at S&K Hotels. Please note that limited accommodation for guests' drivers and chauffeurs is available for an additional fee.
